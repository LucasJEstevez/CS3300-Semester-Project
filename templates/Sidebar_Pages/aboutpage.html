About

Welcome to 1-Stop Car Shop! This free software combines “all present market listings” (simulated data) for new and used cars onto one website (by means of a large, constantly updating database of cars) with recently sold car prices included (if applicable) and provides a way to have your car’s price estimated instantly. Save time by using the functionalities of our web service, rather than finding each function of the service on a different site!

This web service was created mainly as a team-based class project for CS3300 at the University of Colorado Colorado Springs by Computer Science students John Camargo, Jackson Seales, Kylie Sanchez, and Lucas Estevez

Why 1-Stop Car Shop?

  • On automobile shopping sites, of which there are many, browsing is tedious as listings are not shared
  • Many options exist for sites to use when pricing (or getting an offer for) an existing car
  • Generally little to no information on recently sold vehicles (i.e. price, most recent sale location)
